Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new asset or liability?

To add a new asset or liability, go to the Net Worth tab and tap the "+" button. Select the type (asset or liability), enter a name, category, and amount, then tap "Save".

How do I create a budget category?

In the Budget tab, tap "Add Category" and enter the name and monthly budget amount for that category. You can create categories for both income and expenses.

How do I track my expenses?

To track an expense, go to the Budget tab, select the appropriate category, tap "Add Transaction", enter the amount and description, then save. The app will automatically update your budget progress.

How do I upgrade to premium features?

To access premium features, tap on the "Upgrade" button in the Settings tab. You can choose between monthly or annual subscription options.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your financial data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We take your privacy seriously and do not collect personal financial information.
